Engineered Wood

Plywood

Plywood is manufactured by stacking & bonding plies of tropical wood veneers together. We offer an extensive range of plywood from Indonesia, Malaysia and China, including heavy, light, WBP, PVC laminated, polyester, water resistant plywood for every structural and carpentry application.

We offer plywood in an extensive range of thickness from 3mm to 25mm.

Blockboard

Blockboard is made by placing strips of wood in between layers of plywood, and then glued under high pressure. It is lighter in weight than plywood, but is less prone to bending. Hence it is often the preferred material for furniture, wall panels and doors.

Specifications
Thickness 15mm, 18mm, 25mm
Size 4ft x 8 ft

MDF

MDF stands for Medium Density Fireboard, and is a dense engineered composite of small wood fibers pressed at high temperatures. The smooth surface causes MDF to be a popular choice for projects that need to be finished with paintwork.

Marine Plywood

Marine-grade plywood is a good quality and durable plywood option for outdoor projects, especially in environments that are exposed to prolonged humidity. Our marine plywood is BS 1088 compliant, which is the British Standard specification that applies to plywood produced with untreated tropical hardwood veneers, and have a set level of resistance to fungal attack.
