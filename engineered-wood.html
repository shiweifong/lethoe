Engineered Wood

Plywood

Plywood is manufactured by stacking & bonding plies of tropical wood veneers together. We offer an extensive range of plywood from Indonesia, Malaysia and China, including heavy, light, WBP, PVC laminated, polyester, water resistant plywood for every structural and carpentry application.

We offer plywood in an extensive range of thickness from 3mm to 25mm.

Specifications

  • Thickness: 3mm, 5.5mm, 9mm, 12mm, 15mm, 18mm, 25mm
  • Size: 4Ft x 8Ft

Please contact us if you require specific size dimensions that are not listed here.
